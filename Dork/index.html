OATMEAL RECIPE

This is a basic but delicious recipe for oatmeal for all ages

This image was taken by me at home

Here is the list of ingredients required:

  • 100g oatmeal
  • 300ml oat milk
  • 3 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 4 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions:

    1.Pour milk into a pot and boil.
    2.Once the milk boils, reduce the heat and gently add oatmeal while stirring it using a wooden spoon at best.
    3.Once oatmeal has increased in size, add your peanut butter and continue to stir
    4.Add the rest of your ingredients and continue to stir until you have your good blend of ingredients
    5.Serve at best with blueberries to give your meal a good alkalinized balance.
    6.Guten!!
